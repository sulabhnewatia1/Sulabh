About me

From the classroom to the boardroom

A decade of turning rigorous analysis into measurable business outcomes.

I started my career in risk advisory, learning that the best strategies are built on rigorous foundations — not assumptions. That early discipline in financial controls and audit shaped how I approach every engagement: with precision, accountability, and a relentless focus on outcomes.

At Britannia Industries and Swiss Singapore, I moved into corporate finance and operations, where I learned that strategy without execution is just a slide deck. I built teams, managed large-scale manufacturing finances worth $50M, and redesigned supply chains that unlocked $75M in investment — all while keeping one eye on the bigger picture.

Today, as a Project Leader at BCG Dubai, I work with C-suite executives and government regulators across the GCC on their most complex growth challenges — from national economic planning and bank transformations to AI strategy and $200B investment programs.

I hold an MBA from Georgetown University (Top 5% of cohort), am a CFA Charterholder (Top 10% globally), and a Chartered Accountant with All India Rank 6 — a credential combination held by fewer than a few hundred professionals globally.
