About

From the classroom
to the boardroom

I started in risk advisory — learning that the best strategies are built on rigorous foundations, not assumptions. Early discipline in financial controls and audit shaped how I approach every engagement: with precision and a relentless focus on outcomes.

At Britannia and Swiss Singapore, I learned that strategy without execution is just a slide deck. I built teams, oversaw $50M manufacturing operations, and redesigned supply chains that unlocked $75M in investment.

Today at BCG Dubai, I advise C-suite executives and GCC regulators on their most complex challenges — from national economic planning to $200B investment programs to AI strategy.

I hold an MBA from Georgetown University (Top 5%), am a CFA Charterholder, and a Chartered Accountant with All India Rank 6 — a combination held by fewer than ~200 professionals globally.
